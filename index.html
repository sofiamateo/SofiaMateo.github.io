School of Information Technology

The School of Information Technology caters the highest percentage of the student population. Its separation from the Department of Engineering and Technology was approved by the BOT on May 13, 2008. As a separate department, it is manned by seven regular instructors, two temporary status and two contract of service instructors back then. Headed by their Dean, Dr. Mayreen V. Amazona, it continues to provide quality trainings needed by the students.

Mission


ASCOT shall produce globally-competitive, ethically-upright and gender-responsive graduates in the fields of arts and sciences, forestry, agriculture, education, information technology, industrial technology, engineering, marine and environmental sciences by promoting quality instruction, research, extension and production towards sustainable development.


Vision

A Globally-recognized Scientific and Technological University by 2027


Core Values

ACCOUNTABILITY: We value and give emphasis on responsibility to remain a solvent institution. We act with honesty, integrity and thoughtfulness. We demand for compiliance with statutes and regulations; adhere to professional norm and accountable for students' learning and to the general public.
SERVICE ORIENTED: We are engaged to service- whether it is academic, professional, or communal; it supports the other. We support and share. responsibility with the members of our organization. We train our clients in ordrr to be able to integrate and to be competitive on the labor market, must keep pace with changes in the global economy and in technology.
CULTURE OF EXCELLENCE: We encourage every personnel to excel in every performance of his/her duties leading to quality results. We also strive to provide our community with highest possible quality of client services, accessible facilities, products, programs, and technology. The categories of excellence such as well-being, curriculum being assessment and reporting, student performance measure and organizational culture are promoted.
OUTCOMES-BASED -we focus on the outcomes of every service we deliver. In the process, the teacher, parents, students ang shareholders are involved in establishing the outcomes, students are to demonstrate with careful planning.
TRANSPARENTCY -We adhere to provide a complete visibility of all transactions into the successes and sometimes failures of our organization . It is an integral part of our culture as we collaborate and work together towards a common goal , We strive to train management committees , faculty, staff and interested parties on how to access data (online and offline) that can be used to demand accountability .
